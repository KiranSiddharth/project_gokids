About Therapic Kids

AI-Powered Pediatric Rehabilitation for a Better Future

Our Mission

We are dedicated to helping children with motor difficulties through engaging rehabilitation experiences using state-of-the-art AI and gesture-controlled therapy games. Our goal is to make physical therapy an exciting and empowering journey.

Our Vision

To make accessible, technology-driven therapy available to children everywhere. We envision a world where high-quality rehabilitation is not limited by geography or costly equipment, bringing hope and healing to homes globally.
