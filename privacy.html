Introduction

At Therapic Kids, we are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users, particularly the children who engage with our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, store, and utilize data to ensure a safe, secure, and beneficial rehabilitation experience.

Information We Collect

To provide personal and effective therapy programs, we collect the following types of information when necessary:

  • Usage data: Including frequency of login, session duration, and game progression.
  • Device information: Information related to your browser and hardware capabilities necessary for rendering the AI gesture controls.
  • Therapy interaction data: Information detailing therapy sessions, gesture accuracy, and developmental milestones (no videos are saved).

How We Use Information

The information we collect is strictly used to shape a better experience. We use this information to:

  • Improve our therapy tools and AI monitoring software.
  • Personalize rehabilitation, altering the difficulty of games based on the child's recorded progression.
  • Improve system performance and squash bugs within the platform.

Data Protection

All sensitive information, including account details and progression data, is safeguarded using modern encryption standards. Data is safely stored in encrypted databases to prevent unauthorized access. In terms of therapy session data, we process visual gestures locally within the browser context—never capturing or storing video feeds on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Because our platform is fundamentally built for children under 12, we observe enhanced safety measures. Accounts are exclusively managed by adults (parents or licensed therapists) and no personal identifiable tracking apart from the registered adult's details is exposed or shared publicly. We proudly comply with major child online protection regulations.

Third Party Services

We may use secured third-party analytics tools merely to evaluate platform performance, crashes, and aggregated non-identifiable usage statistics. These services are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, your personal data, or if you would like to initiate account deletion, please check our Contact Us page.
